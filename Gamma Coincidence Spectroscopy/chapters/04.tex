\section{Energy Calibration}
To correctly scale the energy axes of the spectra for later sources, an energy calibration is performed for both detectors. It is done by measuring the photopeak positions of $^{22}$Na, $^{137}$Cs, and $^{57}$Co sources and plotting them over the well known theoretical values of the corresponding transitions, giving a linear fit. These fits for the NaI scintillator and the Ge semiconductor detector respectively are depicted in figs.
